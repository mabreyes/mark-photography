The photographer

Mark Rodriguez is a photographer based in Cagayan de Oro. Having done both journalistic and creative projects in the past, his interest in photography is to capture moments and stories worth celerbating.

Passion in photography

For nearly five years now, Mark finds way to continually hone his skills in photography. He has conducted photo coverage of special events and celebration, and has been part of documentation teams of numerous esteemed organizations. In his spare time, he explores his creative side through free photography sessions. This is one of the numerous passion projects that he has to experiment various techniques in photography.

evolving

As the photography industry continues to grow competitive throughout the years, Mark keeps up with the times by seeking new styles and techniques to improve his skills in photography.
