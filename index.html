About Mark

Learn more about Mark's passion in photography as well as his philosophy and steps to succeed in pursuing passion in photography.

The photographer

Mark Rodriguez is a photographer based in Cagayan de Oro. He is a very passionate photographer and has done a lot of photography projects.

His passion in photography

For almost five years, Mark has continue to pursue his passion in photography. He sometimes do photography sessions for free because of this.

Discover to improve oneself

As the photography industry has continued to be competitive throughout the years, he discovers more tools to improve his skills in photography.

Mark's steps to success

As Mark discovers and improve himself to be a better photographer, he believes that being humble and sharing your skills are vital keys to success.
